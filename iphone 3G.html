Facts

The iPhone 3G is a smartphone designed and marketed by Apple Inc. It is the second generation of iPhone, successor to the original iPhone, and was introduced on June 9, 2008, at the WWDC 2008 which took place at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, United States.The iPhone 3GS had a 3.5-inch display and a stylish, portable design. A camera was contained in its glossy black or white plastic back, and the device was bordered by a silver band. Apple's design heritage was upheld and the iPhone 3GS's appeal was aided by its sleek and portable design.the iPhone 3GS maintained the sleek aesthetics of its predecessor, featuring a 3.5-inch display with a resolution of 320 x 480 pixels.
